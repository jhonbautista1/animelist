Inazuma Eleven Go


Inazuma Eleven GO (2011–2012) – 47 episodes
Set 10 years after the original series, it follows Matsukaze Tenma, a young midfielder, as he joins Raimon Junior High.
The story focuses on the Football Frontier International (FFI) tournament and the struggle against the oppressive Football Frontier organization.

Inazuma Eleven GO: Chrono Stone (2012–2013) – 51 episodes
Tenma and friends travel through time to prevent the Dark Emperors from altering history and erasing football from the world.
Introduces new teammates and advanced techniques, with time-travel twists.

Inazuma Eleven GO: Galaxy (2014–2015) – 43 episodes
Takes the story into space football, where Tenma leads Japan against alien teams in the Grand Celestial Tournament.
Features cosmic-level skills, epic matches, and teamwork challenges on a galactic scale.


Jujutsu Kaisen (TV)


Jujutsu Kaisen Season 1 (2020–2021) – 24 episodes
Introduces Yuji Itadori, a high schooler who swallows a cursed object to save friends.
Joins Tokyo Jujutsu High under Satoru Gojo to fight curses.
Covers the Introduction, Cursed Womb Arc, and Death Painting Arc.

2. Jujutsu Kaisen 0 (Movie) (2021) – Optional prequel
Follows Yuta Okkotsu, a new student at Tokyo Jujutsu High, dealing with his cursed childhood friend.
Serves as a prequel to Season 1, giving context for some characters.

3. Jujutsu Kaisen Season 2 (2023–2024) – 20+ episodes
Covers the Shibuya Incident Arc, one of the darkest and most intense arcs in the series.
Shows major battles, character development, and reveals more about curses and the villain Sukuna.



Tokyo Revengers: Christmas Showdown


okyo Revengers Season 1 (2021) – 24 episodes
Follows Takemichi Hanagaki, a 26-year-old who learns he can travel back in time.
He tries to save his ex-girlfriend Hinata Tachibana from being killed by the Tokyo Manji Gang.
Covers the introduction, forming alliances, and key battles with rival gangs.

2. Tokyo Revengers: Christmas Showdown Arc (Season 2 Part 1) (2023–2024) – ~14 episodes
Focuses on the Christmas Showdown, a pivotal battle between the Tokyo Manji Gang and rival gang Valhalla.
Deepens character relationships and Takemichi’s growth as a leader.

3. Tokyo Revengers: Tenjiku Arc (Season 2 Part 2) (2024–2025)
Covers the fight against the Tenjiku gang, one of the strongest gangs threatening the Tokyo Manji Gang.
High-stakes battles and major character development continue.
